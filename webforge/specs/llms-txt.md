# llms.txt Specification (GEO)

## What is llms.txt?

A plain text file at `/llms.txt` that helps AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini)
understand your website quickly. Think of it as "robots.txt for comprehension" — while robots.txt
controls access, llms.txt aids understanding.

## llms.txt Template

```markdown
# [Site Name]

> [One-line description: what the site is and who it serves]

## About
[2-3 paragraphs explaining:
- What the organization does
- Who it serves
- What makes it unique
- Key value proposition]

## Key Topics
- [Topic 1]: [Brief description]
- [Topic 2]: [Brief description]
- [Topic 3]: [Brief description]
- [Topic 4]: [Brief description]
- [Topic 5]: [Brief description]

## Important Pages
- [Homepage](https://site.com/): [What users find here]
- [About](https://site.com/about): [Description]
- [Services/Products](https://site.com/services): [Description]
- [Contact](https://site.com/contact): [Description]
- [FAQ](https://site.com/faq): [Description]
- [Blog](https://site.com/blog): [Description]

## Products / Services
- [Product/Service 1]: [Description, key features, who it's for]
- [Product/Service 2]: [Description, key features, who it's for]

## Frequently Asked Questions
- Q: [Common question 1]?
  A: [Brief answer]
- Q: [Common question 2]?
  A: [Brief answer]
- Q: [Common question 3]?
  A: [Brief answer]

## Contact

- Phone: [phone]

```

## Best Practices

1. **Keep it current** — Update llms.txt when site content changes significantly
2. **Be factual** — No marketing fluff, just clear information
3. **Be specific** — Exact numbers, dates, and names help AI accuracy
4. **Use markdown** — AI systems parse markdown well
5. **Include URLs** — Full absolute URLs so AI can reference specific pages
6. **Answer likely questions** — Think about what users would ask AI about your site
7. **Cite sources** — If you mention statistics, indicate the source
8. **Entity clarity** — If your name is similar to other entities, disambiguate
